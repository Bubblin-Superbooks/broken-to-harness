"Isn't this our man—Beresford?"

"Of course it is, and there's Mr. Schröder close by him. We'll go up and make our salaams."

So the young men wound through the crowd, and were very cordially received by Mrs. Schröder, and indeed by Mr. Beresford. For the Commissioner knew his popularity in the Office and was pleased at it, and was always glad to meet decent-looking men belonging to it in society.
